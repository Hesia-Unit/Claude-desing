The context

Three realities are reshaping drone security.

European critical operators, defense programs, and drone integrators all face converging pressures on firmware integrity, data confidentiality, and supply-chain trust.

01 · Regulation

NIS2 is live.

Over 100 000 European critical operators must now secure their OT and IoT fleets — including industrial drones. The Cyber Resilience Act enters force in 2027.

02 · Cryptographic debt

Quantum computers are coming.

By 2030, today's RSA and ECC cryptography will start falling. NIST's August 2024 standards (FIPS 203, 204, 205) are the new baseline. Waiting is not a strategy.

03 · Supply chain

Sovereignty is non-negotiable.

ITAR, EAR, opaque vendors. Europe's critical infrastructure needs alternatives — built at home, auditable end-to-end, free of geopolitical kill-switches.

The product

One secure firmware stack.
Four building blocks.

HESIA is a software suite that sits between your drone's operating system and its communication stack. We integrate with PX4, ArduPilot, ROS 2 and custom Linux on ARM64.

C

HESIA Core

Drone firmware hardened with post-quantum key agreement, signature verification, and TEE-anchored identity.

ML-KEM-1024 · ML-DSA-87 · AES-256-GCM · OP-TEE
S

HESIA Command

Ground control server with mutual TLS 1.3, Ed25519-signed policy, and allowlist verification at session start.

TLS 1.3 mTLS · Ed25519 · RFC 5705 exporter
V

HESIA Observe

End-to-end encrypted video channel with per-frame authenticated encryption and streaming policy enforcement.

AEAD per-frame · stream policy · replay window
A

HESIA Attest

Remote attestation with hardware-bound keys anchored in Tegra fuse or external secure element. Coming Q2 2027.

Fuse-bound P-384 · measured boot · RATS

Our foundations

Built on standards. Audited. Documented.

Cryptography is too important for shortcuts. Every primitive we use is standardized, every choice is documented, every implementation is reviewed.

NIST FIPS

ML-KEM-1024 (FIPS 203), ML-DSA-87 (FIPS 204), SHA-3 (FIPS 202), HMAC-SHA256 (FIPS 198-1).

ANSSI ROADMAP

On track for CSPN certification in 2027. Common Criteria EAL4+ targeted Q4 2028.

LIBRARIES

Cryptographic primitives via OpenSSL 3.x EVP and the vetted liboqs post-quantum library.

TEE & HARDWARE

Trusted Application on OP-TEE 4.x, sealing and attestation. External secure-element option for long-term device binding.
